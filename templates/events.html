Events organized by SIMA

SIMA has been known for hosting engaging student events which span from dean town halls, to game nights, to diversity equity & inclusion talks, to festive events. SIMA receives a fund from the administration every term to host these events. SIMA officers, esp. the Event Coordinator, organize these events, manage the logistics and have a good time with all the UMSI cohort. Some of our friends outside SIMA also help clean up afterwards (thanks guys!).
